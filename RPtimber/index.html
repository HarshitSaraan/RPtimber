Welcome

Welcome to RP TIMBERS Pvt Ltd. We specialize in importing high-quality teakwood, hardwood, pinewood logs from around the world and shaping them into precision sawn timber for India’s wholesale market. With years of experience in the timber industry, we serve traders, builders, architects, and interior professionals who value reliable quality and responsible sourcing.

Our expertise extends beyond raw timber. We have recently introduced a curated range of easy-to-install wooden flooring and wall panels, designed to bring natural warmth and timeless elegance to modern spaces.

We take pride in building long-term relationships through quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction. Visit our facilities or connect with our team to experience the craftsmanship and character that define RP Timbers.
